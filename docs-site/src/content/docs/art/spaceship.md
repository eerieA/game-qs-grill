There will be only one spaceship, that of Phineas', where the emporium also lives.

# Spaceship

Has functional areas like

- Bridge
- Captain’s cabin
- Diner & casino
- Kitchen
- Hydroponics bay
- Engine room
- Cargo hold
- Bathroom
- Airlock
.
